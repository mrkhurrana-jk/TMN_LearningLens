Who we are...



A sister concern of the Creative Academy, we have been setting careers since the last 15 years. While shallow promises of entrance coaching were not enough, we took a step and understood the hassles of travelling to different classes for different exams. We offer teaching, training & preparation under one roof for all competitive entrance exams. Everyone can be an advisor, mentor, teacher or a counsellor but we befriend the student and grasp their difficulties better through personalized attention. As friends we can not only solve their academic issues but also their adolescent issues.
